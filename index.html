About Parmesan Cheese

Parmesan cheese, also known as Parmigiano-Reggiano, is a hard, granular cheese that is produced from cow's milk and has a rich and savory flavor. It is named after the areas of Parma, Reggio Emilia, and Modena, as well as parts of Bologna and Mantua, Italy.
